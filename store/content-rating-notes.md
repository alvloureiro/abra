# Content rating questionnaire — Abra

Reference answers for IARC / Play Console content rating.

## App type

- Books & Reference / Education tool
- Utility for listening to user-imported documents

## User-generated content

- **Yes** — users import their own PDF files
- Content is **not** hosted, moderated, or shared by the app
- No social features, reporting, or public feeds

## Violence, sexuality, language, controlled substances

- **None** in the app itself
- User-imported PDF content is outside developer control; the app is a neutral reader

## Interactive elements

- No user-to-user interaction
- No unrestricted internet access from the app (no `INTERNET` permission)
- No in-app purchases (v1)
- No ads

## Expected rating

Typically **Everyone / 3+** for the app mechanism. UGC disclosure may add questionnaire items but should not raise the rating for the app binary itself.

## Store listing note

Consider one line in the full description: "You import your own documents; Abra does not provide or review book content."
