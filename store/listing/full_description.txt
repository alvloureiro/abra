Abra turns your PDF ebooks into audio you can listen to anywhere — privately, on your device.

Import PDFs from your phone or cloud storage, and Abra extracts the text and reads it aloud using your device's text-to-speech voices. Pick up where you left off with automatic progress tracking.

<b>Private by design</b>
• No account required
• No ads or analytics
• Your files stay on your device
• No unnecessary permissions

<b>Built for listening</b>
• Import PDFs with the system file picker
• Play, pause, and resume listening
• Choose your preferred TTS voice and language
• Mini player stays visible while you browse your library

<b>Simple library</b>
• See all imported ebooks in one place
• Open any book and continue from your last position

<b>Limitations</b>
• Text-based PDFs only — scanned pages without selectable text are not supported
• Encrypted PDFs are not supported

Abra is open source. Questions and feedback welcome on GitHub.
